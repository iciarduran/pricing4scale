// The Problem

Most Businesses
Price by Instinct.
That's Expensive.

Pricing decisions are often made once — at launch — then forgotten. The result: revenue that doesn't match your value, customers who don't understand what they're paying for, and growth that plateaus.

Pricing4Scale gives you a systematic way out: a diagnosis that exposes the real problem, tools that quantify the answer, and a playbook to execute it.

You're pricing from cost, not value

Margins tell you your floor — not what the market will actually pay. Leaving that gap unexplored is the most common revenue leak we see.

Your packaging doesn't guide the buyer

If customers can't quickly see which option is theirs, they hesitate. Hesitation kills conversion — in e-commerce, services, and B2B alike.

No clear path to higher-value customers

Without deliberate tier design, you serve whoever shows up. With it, you attract the segment willing to pay most — and retain them longest.

Market entry is a guessing game

Setting prices for a new market, product line, or geography without data and structure leads to either money left on the table or deals lost to price.

Honest Answers

Is this only for tech or SaaS companies?+
Not at all. Pricing4Scale was built to work across industries — product businesses, service firms, e-commerce, manufacturing, professional services. The principles of value capture apply everywhere price is involved.
What does the free diagnosis actually give me?+
You'll fill out a structured Google Form covering how you currently price, what your customers look like, and how deals typically close. Based on your answers, you'll get a summary of your pricing model's strengths, weaknesses, and the #1 thing to fix first.
When will the paid tools be available?+
We're building them now. Join the waitlist on this page and you'll be the first to know — plus early access pricing. The Market Entry Calculator is the next in the pipeline.
How does consulting work?+
Send us an email at pricing4scale@gmail.com describing your business and pricing challenge. We'll reply with a few questions, then scope a proposal tailored to what you actually need — no generic packages, no pressure.
Can I use the tools without hiring a consultant?+
Absolutely — that's the point. The tools are designed to be self-serve for founders and operators who want to move fast without a consultant. Consulting is an option, not a requirement.
What if I just launched and have no pricing data yet?+
The Diagnosis and Market Entry Calculator are specifically useful at this stage — they help you set an informed launch price rather than guessing. Pre-revenue is actually the best time to get pricing right.
